Spiced Maple Wine
=================

For a 1 gallon batch: 

- 1 gallon water
- maple syrup to 1.090
- 1 tbsp rainbow pepper blend
- 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek
- 1/4 tsp wine tannin
- Mangrove Jack M02 cider yeast

Post fermentation:
- 1/4 tsp citric acid
- 10oz Maple syrup back sweetening
- bottle + pasteurize
