Makgeolli
=========

- 240g glutinous rice
- 200g non-glutinous rice flour
- 60g nuruk
- M02 cider yeast (pinch)
- 2500ml water

  Instructions
  ------------

  1. Boil the rice with 1/2 the water for 30 minutes.
  2. Cool with the other half of the water.
  3. Add the flour.
  4. Cool to pitch temp.
  5. Add nuruk and yeast
