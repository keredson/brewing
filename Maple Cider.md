Maple Cider
===========

For a 1 gallon batch: 

- 1 gallon apple juice (Tree Top 3 apple blend)
- maple syrup to 1.090 (30oz)
- 1 tbsp rainbow pepper blend
- 1/2 teaspoon Fenugreek
- 1/4 tsp wine tannin
- Mangrove Jack M02 cider yeast
- Fermaid O

Possible Post fermentation:
- 1/4 tsp citric acid
- 10oz Maple syrup back sweetening
- bottle + pasteurize
